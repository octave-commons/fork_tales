# Dialog — River Gate

**Mage of Receipts:** You want the world to remember you kindly.  
**Keeper of Receipts:** I want the world to remember *accurately*.  

**Mage:** Accuracy is a weapon.  
**Keeper:** So is forgetting.  

**Mage:** Then what do we pay?  
**Keeper:** The fork tax. Not to punish choice—  
to prove we chose.

**Mage:** And η?  
**Keeper:** η is what sees you back when you pretend you’re alone.
