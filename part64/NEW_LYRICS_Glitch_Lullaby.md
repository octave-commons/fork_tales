[Lead - Whispered]
Lay your ear on the riverbed.
Click. Click. Click.
Receipts like pebbles.
領収書は 小石のように。

[Choir - Stutter]
An-anchor.
Tax. Tax.
F-f-fork.

[Lead]
The night admits it.
夜が認めるまで。
Proof is not steel.
It is a thread.

[Choir - Harmony]
We choose.
We prove.
選ぶ、そして証明する。

[Bridge - Glitch]
P → R → N
Π → A
(Feedback loop intensifies)
η... μ...
Meaning remains broken.
壊れたまま 意味。

[Outro]
Just keep rolling them.
Dice in the dark.
闇の中で ダイスを。
Sleep now, checksum.
おやすみ、チェックサム。
