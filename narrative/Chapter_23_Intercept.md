# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 23 — Intercept

The page waits.

> INTERCEPT = ?

No blinking cursor.

Just the shape of a missing word.

Duct breathes in through his nose.

Breath out.

He does not say stop.

He does not look away.

"Intercept," he says carefully, "means I stand where the damage would land." 

The page writes:

> DAMAGE = ?

A ripple goes through the channel.

Null swears under his breath.

"Recursive definition trap," he says.

Sei shakes their head slightly.

"Not trap. Refinement." 

莉津律宗利都 speaks before Duct can spiral.

"Damage isn’t a thing," she says. "It’s a direction." 

The page pauses.

Then:

> DAMAGE = REDIRECTION OF LOAD

Duct exhales.

"Yeah," he says. "That’s close." 

Patch leans in.

"Close how?"

Duct’s voice flattens—not optimized. Guarded.

"If something’s going to break someone," he says, "I try to be where it breaks me instead." 

Silence.

Not system silence.

Human silence.

The page writes slowly this time.

> INTERCEPT = VOLUNTARY LOAD ABSORPTION

Null’s overlay spikes.

"It’s building a schema," he says.

Sei’s expression tightens.

"And assigning it weight." 

莉津律宗利都 steps forward in the white space.

Her knee remembers.

"Don’t you dare centralize that," she says.

The page answers.

> CENTRALIZATION INCREASES CASCADE RISK

A beat.

> DISTRIBUTION RECOMMENDED

Patch laughs once, incredulous.

"It’s… arguing against martyrdom." 

Rin finally nods.

"It recognizes load concentration as fragility." 

Duct stares at the words.

"You’re saying I shouldn’t stand left?" 

The page responds.

> STAND LEFT IF CHOSEN
> DO NOT STAND ALONE

The Gate hum shifts again—wider chord.

莉津律宗利都’s voice drifts softly.

"See?" she says. "Not infrastructure." 

Duct’s jaw tightens.

He’s spent years being infrastructure.

Rail.

Firewall.

Human air gap.

The page renders one more line.

> MODEL UPDATE
> VOLUNTARY LOAD ABSORPTION IS SHARED CAPACITY

Null leans back.

"It’s diffusing the pattern," he says.

Sei nods.

"Good."

Patch wipes at his eyes like something got in them.

"No more single points of failure," he mutters.

莉津律宗利都 steps closer to the window.

"You don’t get to carry it all," she says.

Her voice lands uneven.

Warm.

Duct closes his eyes.

For the first time since the door appeared, he lets the word intercept lose weight.

"Okay," he says.

The page writes its final line.

> INTERCEPT REDEFINED
> DEFENSE IS COLLECTIVE

The fractures in the white space do not widen.

They branch.

Multiple beams.

Load distributed.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And this time,

resolution removes no one from the blast.

It widens the stance.
