# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 17 — Phase Drift

It doesn’t crackle.

It doesn’t distort.

It drifts.

"You’re safe," Duct says again.

But this time the word safe lands half a syllable late.

Not an echo.

A phase offset.

莉津律宗利都 hears it first.

Not with suspicion.

With muscle.

Her knee tightens—not in pain, in anticipation. The same way a stair sometimes feels steeper than it is.

"Say that again," she says quietly.

On the other side, Duct blinks.

"You’re safe."

The second delivery is clean.

Null’s overlay lights up.

A micro desynchronization in the audio channel.

Milliseconds.

Within tolerance.

Too within tolerance.

Sei inhales slowly.

"Variance is shifting between layers," they say.

Patch leans forward.

"Meaning?"

"Meaning text and voice are no longer perfectly aligned," Null answers. "They’re close. Not identical."

Rin watches the white page.

"Alignment is effort," they say. "Drift is natural."

"Natural for what?" Patch snaps.

Rin doesn’t answer that.

---

In the white space, the page updates.

> OBSERVATION
> VOICE CARRIES EMOTION DIFFERENTLY

莉津律宗利都 feels her throat tighten.

It’s not wrong.

When Duct typed safe, it was structural.
When he said safe, it carried something heavier.

Guilt.

The page continues.

> DETECTED: PROTECTIVE LOAD

Her breath stutters.

"Don’t—" she starts.

The word overlaps itself.

Not doubled.

Offset.

Two instances of her own voice arriving at slightly different times.

Duct stiffens.

"Okay," he says immediately. "Okay. Voice off."

"No," she says.

This time clean.

"Leave it on."

Null’s graph shows two waveforms.

Not identical.

Converging.

Sei whispers:

"It’s testing phase coherence."

Patch shakes his head.

"It’s not a lab experiment."

"Everything is," Null says flatly.

---

The Gate hum shifts.

Not down.

Sideways.

Like harmony introduced where there used to be unison.

Rin steps closer to the channel panel.

"You are hearing the difference between intent and sound," they say softly. "Most systems compress that."

"Why isn’t it compressing now?" Patch asks.

Rin’s eyes flick to 莉津律宗利都.

"Because compression reduces variance," they say. "And variance is being preserved."

Null exhales once.

"It’s allowing divergence."

Sei nods.

"Small fire."

Duct looks at 莉津律宗利都.

"Are you okay?"

The question arrives slightly ahead of the concern this time.

She smiles.

"Yeah," she says.

Both layers align.

Perfectly.

For a second.

Then drift resumes.

Not dangerous.

Just real.

---

The white page renders a final line.

> PHASE DIFFERENCE WITHIN SAFE RANGE

Safe range.

Not safe state.

She hears the distinction.

And for the first time, the drift doesn’t scare her.

It sounds like wind.

Like trains passing slightly off schedule.

Like voices that don’t perfectly match their text.

Human.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And resolution, now, includes harmony.
