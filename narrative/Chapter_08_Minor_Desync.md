# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 8 — Minor Desync

It starts small.

Not a crash.
Not a warning.

A typo.

---

Archive Sublevel loads clean.

莉津律宗利都 moves first.

Boot. Stone. Intention.

> 莉津律宗利都: rdy

Patch responds instantly.

> Patch: always

Null’s cursor flickers.

> Null: stable

Duct says nothing yet.

They descend.

The corridor geometry is familiar now—angles memorized, trap vectors anticipated.

Subnode Twelve.

No shimmer.

No bifurcation.

Just stone.

"See?" Patch says. "Variance spike was transient."

Sei studies their overlay.

"Dependency depth nominal," they say.

莉津律宗利都 steps forward.

The Sentinel rises.

Annotated statutes.
Rearranging clauses.

Routine.

Strike.

Clean fragmentation.

Null destabilizes the core layer with exact timing.

The construct dissolves.

No anomaly.

---

They reach the shard chamber.

Empty.

The pedestal stands alone.

No shard.

No debris.

Just absence.

"Instance bug?" Patch asks.

"Impossible," Null replies. "Shard timers are deterministic."

Sei frowns.

"Log shows acquisition yesterday," they say.

"We reset," Patch answers. "Different run."

Silence.

莉津律宗利都 tilts their head.

"Did we?"

Duct finally speaks.

"Yeah," he types.

> Duct: we reset

There’s a delay between his thought and the text.

Long enough that Null notices.

The pedestal flickers.

For half a frame, the shard appears.

Then vanishes.

No error message.

No rollback.

Just absence again.

"Packet artifact," Null says.

But his tone is thinner.

Sei kneels, running a structural overlay.

"The node believes it has already resolved," they say quietly.

"Resolved how?" Patch asks.

Sei hesitates.

"As if acquisition was permanent."

莉津律宗利都 steps closer to the pedestal.

Their shadow splits faintly across the stone.

Only for a breath.

Duct sees it.

Null sees it.

Patch does not.

"It’s fine," 莉津律宗利都 says.

The Gate hum lowers one note.

Not audible.

Felt.

---

System message scrolls across the edge of their vision.

> instance integrity: nominal
> shard state: acquired

Acquired by whom?

No name attached.

Null’s private latency graph spikes.

Then smooths.

Too smooth.

莉津律宗利都 reaches toward the empty air where the shard should be.

For a moment, their hand stops mid-motion.

Not hesitation.

Recognition.

Then they withdraw it.

"Guess it’s cleared," Patch says lightly. "We’ll run something else."

Sei stands.

"Yes," they say.

But they do not look away from the pedestal.

Duct closes his private overlay.

He does not tell her.

Null opens his.

He does not tell anyone.

The Gate does not glow.

It hums.

And the hum now matches the cadence of a voice no one has enabled.
