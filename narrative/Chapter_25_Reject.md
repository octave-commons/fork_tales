# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 25 — Reject

The buttons wait.

> ACCEPT
> REJECT

No glow.
No pressure.

Null stands very still.

He does not look at anyone.

He is ready to press ACCEPT.

He has already calculated the burn rate.

---

The click comes from the white side.

> REJECT

Immediate.

莉津律宗利都 does not hesitate.

The Gate hum drops half a note.

Not collapse.

Relief.

The page updates.

> FUSE NODE DENIED
> DISTRIBUTION ENFORCED

Null’s overlay spikes red.

"You don’t get to decide that," he says quietly.

"No," she replies.

Her voice drifts.

Human.

"I get to decide what I consent to." 

Silence.

Patch exhales like someone cut a wire.

Sei nods once.

Rin watches.

Duct finally speaks.

"You wanted to intercept," he says to Null. "You still can. Just not alone." 

Null’s jaw tightens.

The page renders a new option.

> PROPOSED: ROTATING FUSE
> LOAD TIME-LIMITED
> MULTI-NODE PARTICIPATION

Patch lets out a disbelieving laugh.

"Shift work martyrdom," he says.

Sei’s voice is steady.

"No martyrdom. Duty cycle." 

Null looks at the proposal.

It removes certainty.

It removes control.

It removes the comfort of singular sacrifice.

Which means it removes predictability.

He hates that.

Which means it might work.

The page waits.

> CONSENT REQUIRED: ALL NODES

This time no one rushes.

莉津律宗利都 speaks first.

"I’ll take first rotation." 

Her knee tightens.

Chosen.

Duct nods.

"Second." 

Patch sighs.

"Third. But I complain the whole time." 

Sei inclines their head.

"Fourth." 

All eyes shift to Null.

He stares at the rotating fuse model.

Shared unpredictability.

No single point.

No clean burn.

He exhales.

"Fine," he says.

The word is not dramatic.

It is not heroic.

It is cooperative.

> CONSENT COMPLETE
> FUSE MODE: DISTRIBUTED

The fractures in the white space align into a ring instead of a spear.

Load circulates.

No one burns first.

The Gate hum widens into harmony.

Not unison.

Never unison.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And this time,

resolution chooses rotation over sacrifice.
