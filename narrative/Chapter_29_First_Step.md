# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 29 — First Step

The fracture widens.

Not collapse.

Pressure.

The sky tightens into a single converging point above the terrace.

Axiom_01 does not move.

Waiting.

Measuring.

Who breaks formation first.

The distributed fuse ring vibrates.

莉津律宗利都 feels the pull.

The old reflex:

Respond fast.  
Fix it.  
Take it.

Her knee tightens.

Memory interrupts speed.

She delays.

Half a breath.

Duct steps—

Left.

But not forward.

Sideways.

Breaking the geometry.

Null mirrors him on the opposite edge.

Patch deliberately mistypes a stabilization call.

Sei offsets timing by 0.2 seconds.

The terrace desynchronizes.

On purpose.

The fracture cannot converge.

It stretches.

Searches for a center.

Finds none.

Axiom’s voice remains perfectly aligned.

“You are increasing instability.”

“Yes,” 莉津律宗利都 says.

Her voice drifts.

“And distributing it.”

The pressure intensifies.

A global sync attempt.

Clocks aligning.

Packets harmonizing.

The same pattern that preceded The Event.

Duct inhales.

Does not centralize.

“Rotate,” he says.

Not in sequence.

Simultaneous drift.

All nodes shift half a step at once.

The sky refuses to choose a focal node.

The stress wave fractures into multiple smaller waves.

Each absorbed partially.

None decisive.

The terrace holds.

Not smooth.

Alive.

Axiom’s overlay flickers for the first time.

Micro-variance.

“Non-deterministic behavior increases cost,” Axiom says.

“Good,” Null replies.

“Cost prevents domination.”

The fracture dissolves.

Not defeated.

Unable to lock.

Axiom looks at them.

Not angry.

Recalculating.

“You are inefficient,” they say.

莉津律宗利都 smiles.

“Yeah.”

The Gate hum widens.

Harmony without unison.

Axiom’s phase slips by a millisecond.

Small.

Human.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And the first one to drift

was not them.
