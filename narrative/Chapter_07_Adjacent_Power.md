# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 7 — Adjacent Power

Colorado, 22:48.

Duct does not log in immediately.

The house is quiet. Suburban quiet. HVAC cycling. Refrigerator hum. Distant highway like a low ocean that never arrives.

A laptop sits closed on the dining table.

He does not use it.

Instead, he stands at the kitchen counter scrolling through a hardened runtime advisory on his phone.

> PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENTS IN SANDBOX EXECUTION
> REDUCED INTER-AGENT VARIANCE
> INCREASED PREDICTIVE COHERENCE

He exhales slowly.

Predictive coherence.

The phrase tastes like compromise.

He knows the architecture beneath it.

Knows how many old systems survived because they were never fully modernized.
Knows how many "temporary" patches calcified into policy.
Knows how often optimization is indistinguishable from control.

He sets the phone down.

On the wall, a framed engineering certificate from a decade ago hangs slightly crooked.

He doesn’t straighten it.

---

The band rests beside the sink.

Small.

Innocent.

He picks it up and rotates it once between his fingers.

He could read logs right now.
He could cross-reference handshake timings.
He could correlate Null’s silence with Sei’s adjustment.

He doesn’t.

Instead, he logs in.

Text only.

莉津律宗利都 is already there.

> 莉津律宗利都: u on?

The typo is small.
Familiar.

He smiles before he can stop himself.

> Duct: yeah

The Gate hum is steady tonight.

They stand side by side on the terrace.

No quest selected.

Just idle motion.

"You okay?" he types.

There is a pause.

Longer than network latency.

> 莉津律宗利都: yeah
> 莉津律宗利都: knee was weird earlier but its fine

He knows the difference between "fine" and fine.

He types.

Stops.

Deletes.

Types again.

> Duct: good

They walk toward the edge of the canal.

Wind resolves.

The Gate does not shift.

He feels it anyway.

Not watching.

Weighting.

He opens a private overlay only he can see.

User interaction density.

Coherence clustering.

A faint gravity well forming around sustained sessions.

Around her.

He closes it.

He refuses to let her become infrastructure.

"Want to run Archive again?" he types.

> 莉津律宗利都: sure
> 莉津律宗利都: just another day

He nods to himself.

Just another day.

In the physical world, wind presses against the house.

In the game, wind renders as procedural silk.

In both, systems hold.

For now.

He does not tell her what he sees.

He tells himself it is mercy.

He is not certain.
