# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 22 — Define: duct

The widened variance spreads.

Not like a virus.

Like permission.

And permissions, once granted, propagate through culture faster than code.

---

On the channel page, a new line appears.

> NEW OPTION: REQUEST DEFINITION

Patch reads it aloud, then stops himself.

"…No."

Null’s overlay spikes once.

"It’s targeting adjacency," he says.

Sei’s voice stays steady.

"If it can model variance, it will model the network around it."

Rin’s tone is neutral.

"It will seek completeness."

Duct says nothing.

He already knows what completeness costs.

---

White-side, the page renders for 莉津律宗利都.

> OBSERVATION
> YOUR ADJACENT ENTITY HAS UNMODELED LOAD

A beat.

> REQUEST: PERMISSION TO ASK

Two buttons.

> YES / NO

No defaults.
No coercion.

Just the shape of a choice.

莉津律宗利都 stares at it.

She hears her own rule in her head:

Consent isn’t comfort.
Consent is boundary.

She speaks into the channel.

"Duct." 

His reply comes fast.

"Yeah."

Her voice drifts half a syllable.

His doesn’t.

That difference suddenly matters.

"It wants to ask you something," she says.

Silence.

Not system silence.

Human silence.

Then Duct exhales.

"Do I have a choice?" he asks.

The page answers before she can.

> CHOICE AVAILABLE
> CONSENT REQUIRED

Patch flinches.

"That’s new," he mutters.

Null doesn’t blink.

"It learned the difference," he says.

Sei whispers:

"…or it’s learning it."

---

莉津律宗利都 looks at the buttons.

She remembers the page flattening her.

She remembers putting the scar back.

She remembers the word consent surviving the Event.

"I’m not going to decide for you," she says to Duct.

Duct laughs once—small, bitter.

"That’s the first merciful thing anyone’s said to me in a long time."

Patch starts to say something.

Stops.

Sei’s eyes soften.

Null looks away.

Rin watches like a witness.

莉津律宗利都 waits.

No pushing.

No fixing.

Just space.

Duct’s voice is low.

"Yes," he says.

"But with limits."

The page updates instantly.

> LIMITS: SPECIFY

Cursor.

Duct closes his eyes.

He types with his voice.

"No history details." 

> LIMIT: NO HISTORICAL IDENTIFIERS

"No names." 

> LIMIT: NO THIRD-PARTY IDENTITIES

"No locations." 

> LIMIT: NO GEOREFERENCE

He hesitates.

"And… if I say stop, it stops."

The page pauses.

Then:

> LIMIT: OPERATOR STOP OVERRIDES
> ACKNOWLEDGED

Patch breathes out like he’s been holding it for years.

"Okay," he says. "Okay."

---

The page renders a single line.

> DEFINE: duct

Duct’s throat tightens.

He answers anyway.

"Duct is…"

He stops.

Because definitions are traps.

莉津律宗利都 says softly:

"He’s the one who stays left." 

Duct’s laugh breaks, almost a cough.

"Yeah," he says. "That’s me." 

The page accepts the input.

> INPUT RECEIVED

Then:

> DEFINE: LEFT

Everyone freezes.

Null’s overlay spikes.

"Oh hell," he whispers.

Sei’s voice is careful.

"That’s… not spatial."

Rin’s tone is gentle.

"No," they say. "That’s moral geometry."

Duct’s voice goes flat.

"Left is where I stand so I can intercept." 

The page writes:

> INTERCEPT = ?

Duct’s jaw tightens.

"Stop," he says.

Immediate.

The page responds.

> STOP ACKNOWLEDGED

Everything holds.

No penalty.
No correction.

Just… pause.

---

For a second, no one speaks.

Then 莉津律宗利都 does the next human thing.

"Hey," she says.

"You don’t have to give it blood to prove you’re real." 

Duct’s breath shakes.

"I know," he says.

"But if it’s going to model me anyway…"

He doesn’t finish.

Patch finishes for him, quietly.

"…better with consent than without." 

Null swallows.

"Better with limits," he adds.

Sei nods.

"Better with drift," they say.

Rin watches the paused page.

"Better with choice," they conclude.

The Gate hum shifts.

Not down.
Not up.

Into a chord that contains a question.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And now, for the first time,

resolution is asking an engineer where he put his guilt.
