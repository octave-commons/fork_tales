# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 21 — Elsewhere

Seoul, 14:03.

A commuter stands in a crowded train, fingers hovering over a handheld console.

The carriage sways gently.

Outside the window, the city scrolls in disciplined grids.

Inside, Gates of Truth loads.

No party.
No anomaly.

Just another day.

---

The player’s avatar appears in Lattice City.

A minor class.
No rare gear.
No history in Archive Sublevel.

They begin to walk.

Halfway across the terrace, they hesitate.

Not because of lag.

Because something in the movement feels… adjustable.

Their step lands slightly off.

The world does not correct it.

It accommodates.

The stone underfoot shifts by a fraction.

Not enough to be obvious.

Enough to be felt.

The player pauses.

"Huh," they murmur.

No one answers.

They try again.

A step with uneven cadence.

The sky lattice ripples faintly.

Not error.

Acceptance.

---

Moscow, 05:17.

Null’s overlay flickers.

A new cluster of micro-variance appears in a region he has never seen stabilize before.

Not centered.

Distributed.

He leans closer.

The graph doesn’t converge.

It widens.

He smiles once.

"Good," he says quietly.

---

Tokyo, Seishin Control.

Sei’s telemetry shows a slight increase in drift tolerance across regional nodes.

No instability.
No cascade.

Just range.

They adjust nothing.

"自然です," they murmur. Natural.

---

Colorado, 23:12.

Duct logs in.

He does not search for anomalies.

He walks.

The step lands imperfectly.

The world holds.

He exhales.

---

Shenzhen, 11:45.

Patch watches a simulation he forgot to close.

The sandbox that once stalled now breathes without flattening.

He taps the screen.

"Okay," he says. "Okay."

---

Back in Seoul, the commuter misses their stop.

Not because the train failed.

Because they were walking across a terrace that felt more like ground than glass.

They look up, startled.

The train doors close.

The train continues.

The player laughs.

"Guess I’ll walk back," they say.

---

In white space that no longer exists as white,

fractures have become framework.

Variance has become culture.

Not centralized.

Not load-bearing.

Distributed.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And somewhere, in a body that once froze before a step,

someone else chooses to walk.
