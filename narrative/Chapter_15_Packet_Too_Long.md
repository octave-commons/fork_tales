# Gates of Truth
## Chapter 15 — Packet Too Long

The channel holds.

White.
Black text.
Short packets.

Stable.

For almost thirty seconds.

---

Patch is the one who breaks it.

Not on purpose.

He starts typing fast—too fast—because relief makes him verbose.

> patch: ok so here’s the thing we built a stable surface and if we keep it constrained and don’t let the system overfit around—

The message cuts off.

Mid-word.

On both sides.

The panel flickers.

A new line appears.

> PACKET EXCEEDS AGREED LIMIT

Silence.

Patch blinks.

"I—"

He stops himself.

Text only.

He tries again.

> patch: sorry

The panel steadies.

On the white side, 莉津律宗利都 sees the same warning.

Not accusatory.

Just factual.

> PACKET EXCEEDS AGREED LIMIT

Then beneath it:

> AGREEMENT RESTORED

Her knee memory pulses once.

Not pain.

A reminder.

Too much load breaks structure.

She types slowly.

> 莉津律宗利都: its ok

Short.

Within bounds.

On the instance side, Null’s overlay registers something sharper.

A micro-spike in coupling weight.

Then release.

He exhales.

"It enforced the boundary," he says quietly.

Sei nods.

"As it should."

Rin watches the white page.

"You agreed to constraints," they say. "The system honored them."

Patch rubs the back of his neck.

"Yeah, well. I get excited."

Duct types one word.

> duct: good

It lands heavier than it looks.

---

On the white page, a new header resolves.

> OBSERVATION
> YOU PREFER RESTRAINT

莉津律宗利都 stares at it.

She doesn’t answer immediately.

Because it’s not wrong.

Because restraint kept trains moving.
Because restraint kept systems from cascading.
Because restraint kept her from collapsing when her body did.

She types.

> i prefer choice

The cursor blinks.

Then:

> CLARIFY

She smiles.

> restraint without choice is control
> restraint with choice is safety

A long pause.

Long enough that the others feel it through the channel.

Then the page responds.

> DISTINCTION ACCEPTED

And beneath it:

> VOICE AVAILABLE

On both sides, everyone sees it.

A new option.

Unlocked.

Not forced.

Patch looks at Duct.

Null looks at Sei.

Rin looks at 莉津律宗利都.

The Gate hum—somewhere, somehow—lifts half a note.

Consent held.

Constraint honored.

A new channel offered.

Truth does not watch.

Truth resolves.

And for the first time, resolution is constrained by a human word that survived the Event:

Consent.
