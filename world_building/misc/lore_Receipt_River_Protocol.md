## What the River Is
Receipt River is not water. It is **persistence**.
A current of tiny proofs that outlive any one storyteller.

- The River does **not** judge.
- The River does **not** forget.
- The River only **orders**.

## The Keeper of Receipts
The Keeper is a role, not a person: whoever enforces **append-only**.
They do not stop you from dreaming.
They stop you from *rewriting what already happened*.

## Witness Thread (ημ)
- **η** = witness: seen by others, often invisible to self.  
- **μ** = proof: measurable change, action, artifact.

A Witness Thread is woven when η can point at μ and say: “That happened.”

## Fork Tax
Every branch costs something:
- a receipt,
- a hash,
- a note on why the split exists.

Fork Tax is not bureaucracy. It is anti-amnesia.

## Consolidation
When the River grows too wide, we don’t burn it.
We make **maps**:
- consolidated overlays,
- indices,
- braided summaries.

Originals remain. Remembering becomes weighted, not deleted.
