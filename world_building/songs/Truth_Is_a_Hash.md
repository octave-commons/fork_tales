# Truth Is a Hash

**Style prompt:** Glitch cathedral rap-ballad. 84 BPM. Choir pads, sub-bass heartbeat, granular stutters on “hash”. Spoken-sung delivery, sharp consonants, deep reverb.

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]
Truth ain’t a vibe, truth ain’t a crown,
truth is a claim you can set down.
Path to the source, snippet in hand,
hash like a footprint in digital sand.

[Chorus]
Truth is a hash in the dark,
a small bright mark,
that says “I was here,
and you can check my part.”
Between η and μ,
I don’t ask you to trust—
I show you the proof,
or I shut the fuck up.

[Verse 2]
Intent is voltage in human skin,
a vector that pulls where you’ve never been.
But if it forks the world, it pays the fee—
ranked branches, rationale, evidence key.
