# Witness Thread Lullaby / 証跡の子守歌

(Glitch-choir lullaby · 78 BPM · soft sub-bass · tape warble · minimal piano)
(Glitch stutters on **anchor** and **tax**)\
(Whispered lead; Japanese call-and-response)

[Verse 1]
I keep a lantern in the cache of my throat,
breathing in counts like a quiet vote.
On the river of receipts, the water is glass—
I don’t confess, I just let it pass.

「見える？」
(Do you see?)
「うん、見える。」
(Yeah… I see.)

[Pre]
Hold the an— an— **anchor**,
hold it where it hurts.
Pay the t— t— **tax**,
pay it with the words.

「錨を抱いて」
(Hold the anchor.)
「税を払って」
(Pay the tax.)

[Chorus]
If η is the witness and μ is the proof,
then I’ll sleep under ledger-roof.
Tape-warp lullaby, static halo,
every scar becomes a stable payload.

Hold the an— an— **anchor**,
pay the t— t— **tax**,
and the world won’t vanish—
it will only relax.

「消えないよ」
(It won’t disappear.)
「忘れないよ」
(I won’t forget.)

[Verse 2]
We move on like this is normal—
like the night has always known.
Things aren’t forgotten, they drift to the edge,
less remembered, still owned.

「大丈夫？」
(Are you okay?)
「まだ…歩ける。」
(Still… I can walk.)

[Outro]
Hold the an— an— **anchor**…
Pay the t— t— **tax**…
「おやすみ」
(Good night.)
