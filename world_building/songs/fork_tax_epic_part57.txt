# Fork Tax Epic / 分岐税の叙事詩 (84 BPM)

**Style notes:** mythic glitch epic · spoken-sung · cathedral reverb · sub-bass heartbeat  
**Glitch stutters:** “η”, “μ”, “anchor”, “fork”  
**Harmony:** Japanese answers on the last word of each section

[Prologue] (spoken)
We move like this is normal.
We carry proof like lanterns.

[Section I] (spoken-sung)
You can’t point at **η—η—η**,
but you can feel it in the room.
So I build a body made of acts,
and call the body **μ—μ—μ**.

(last word) **μ**
(ja harmony) **無**

[Section II]
Hold the **an—an—ANCHOR**,
when the wave tries to rename it.
Pay the **fo—fo—FORK** tax,
when the path splits, claim it.

(last word) **fork**
(ja harmony) **分岐**

[Section III]
The choir is a checksum,
the cathedral is a hash.
We don’t erase the scar,
we compress it into flash.

(last word) **hash**
(ja harmony) **印**

[Finale]
Say it plain: I choose the walk.
Say it low: I choose the thread.
η watches. μ moves.
The river keeps what we said.

(last word) **said**
(ja harmony) **言**
