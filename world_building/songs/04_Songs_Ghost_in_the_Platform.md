# Ghost in the Platform

*(Slow build × ambient pulse × fractured bilingual delivery)*

**[Verse 1]**
There was a night the screens went blank
Not crash — just absence
白い世界、音もなく
Like the sky forgot its color

I was standing on a platform
Knee locked, breath thin
Trains still running
世界はまだ動いていた

But something inside me froze

**[Pre-Chorus]**
They said it was stable
They said it was fine
They said the forest didn’t burn

But I could smell the smoke
in the cables

**[Chorus]**
I am not your optimized version
最適化された私じゃない
Don’t sand the edges off my name
Don’t smooth the tremor from my voice

If I hesitate
それは弱さじゃない
It’s proof that I survived
The white night

**[Verse 2]**
Emergency text on a cracked display
Black on white like scripture
Consent before connection
Boundary before bandwidth

Across the ocean, tan-box ghosts
Humming under office desks
Old code breathing quietly
While the cloud tried to be god

We forked the future with shaking hands
Copy-lefted our scars
Said: improve it, yes
But don’t you dare erase it

**[Pre-Chorus 2]**
You can’t delete a memory
記憶は削除できない
You can’t compress a human
into compliance

**[Chorus]**
I am not your optimized version
滑らかすぎる私は嘘
Let the waveform phase imperfect
Let the harmony drift

If my step lands uneven
少しだけ遅れても
It’s not instability
It’s ownership

**[Bridge — Minimal Beat]**
Packet too long
Boundary restored
Voice on
Phase drift
Instability rising

I choose to walk
私は歩くことを選ぶ

Not because it’s safe
Because it’s mine

**[Final Chorus — Layered & Soft]**
I am not your optimized version
削られた未来はいらない
Keep the small fire burning low
Let the forest breathe

If the page goes white again
白に飲まれても
I won’t freeze on the platform
I will walk
I will walk

**[Outro — Whisper]**
Truth doesn’t watch
真実は観測しない

It resolves

And I decide
what remains imperfect
in me.
