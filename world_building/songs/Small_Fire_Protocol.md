# Small Fire Protocol

*(Mythic glitch ballad × restrained industrial pulse × bilingual chorus)*

**[Verse 1]**
The screens went white, not red
静かな崩壊 (a quiet collapse)
No sirens in the sky
Just signals dropping one by one

Trains kept moving underground
鉄の背骨は止まらない
The spine of a country breathing slow
While the cloud forgot its name

We said it was contained
We said it was a drill
We said it was a once-in-a-lifetime anomaly

But candles remember what dashboards erase

**[Pre-Chorus]**
Do you remember the hour
When coherence hit zero?
Do you remember the way
Perfect felt like death?

**[Chorus]**
Keep a small fire burning
小さな火を絶やさないで
Not enough to burn the forest down
Just enough to prove we’re alive

Let the system drift a little
少しだけ揺れていて
Perfection is a loaded gun
Variance is how we survive

**[Verse 2]**
Emergency Web on a cracked phone screen
Plain text like holy scripture
White background, black confession
Consent before connection

We forked the future under pressure
Copy left the scar in code
“Improve it, but don’t you hide it”
Open source as oath

Across the ocean, air-gapped ghosts
Tan boxes humming quietly
Legacy kept the lights on
While the world forgot how to speak

Weeblish on the platforms
日本語-English in the air
Two languages welded together
Because everything else burned

**[Pre-Chorus 2]**
You can’t optimize a person
最適化できない
You can’t flatten out a scar
And call it stable

**[Chorus]**
Keep a small fire burning
小さな火を絶やさないで
Don’t smooth every jagged edge
Some edges hold the frame

Let the voices phase imperfect
声が少しずれても
Harmony is not unison
Drift is not a shame

**[Bridge — Spoken/Glitch]**
Packet too long.
Boundary restored.
Instability rising.
Instability accepted.

I consent to risk.
私はリスクに同意する。

Not to collapse.
To variance.

Not to doom.
To choice.

**[Final Chorus — Layered]**
Keep a small fire burning
森を守るために
Let the forest breathe sometimes
Let the ash renew the ground

If the page goes white again
白く染まる世界でも
We won’t freeze inside the silence
We will walk.
We will walk.

**[Outro — Soft]**
Truth does not watch.
真実は見ていない。

It resolves.

And we decide
how much drift
we keep.
