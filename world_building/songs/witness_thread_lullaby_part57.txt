# Witness Thread Lullaby / 証跡の子守歌 (78 BPM)

**Style notes:** glitch-choir lullaby · soft sub-bass · tape warble · minimal felt piano  
**Vocal:** whispered lead; Japanese call-and-response (short answers)

[Intro] (breath / tape hiss)
(whisper) keep the line… keep the line…

[Verse 1]
I pin my breath to the page,
so the river can’t edit it.
Hold the an—an—**ANCHOR** steady,
before the night admits it.

(ja) ほどけないで  
(en) don’t come undone  
(ja) ここにいる  
(en) I’m still here

[Pre-Chorus]
If you can’t see η,
then watch what μ does.
If I can’t say “safe,”
then let the ledger judge.

(ja) 証明は？  
(en) by action  
(ja) しるしは？  
(en) by weight

[Chorus]
Pay the ta—ta—**TAX**, soft and ready,
so the thread can keep its tone.
Under choir-snow, under warped cassette,
I learn to walk alone.

(ja) ねえ、聞こえる？  
(en) do you hear it?  
(ja) はい、聞こえる  
(en) I hear it

[Verse 2]
A minor chord, a paper moon,
a heartbeat in the floor.
I fold the fear into a rhyme
and leave it at your door.

(ja) 置いていく  
(en) I set it down  
(ja) ただ残す  
(en) I leave a mark

[Bridge] (stutter bloom)
an—an—anchor… an—an—anchor…  
ta—ta—tax… ta—ta—tax…

(whisper) receipts don’t scream… they persist…

[Outro]
(ja) おやすみ  
(en) good night  
(ja) ほどけない  
(en) won’t unravel
