# Witness Thread Lullaby / 証跡の糸
(Glitch-choir lullaby · 78 BPM · soft sub-bass · tape warble · minimal piano)
CC BY-SA · created with the assistance of an AI

[Verse 1]
Hold the an—an—ANCHOR, hold it steady,
pay the ta—ta—TAX, pay it ready.
There’s a river under everything we say,
and it only keeps what it can replay.

[JP Call]
アンカー　離さないで
税は　静かに払う

[Chorus]
Thread, thread, witness thread,
tie my name to what I did.
If the light goes out, if the story turns,
the ledger doesn’t burn.

[Verse 2]
Tape warble, soft decay,
piano bones in a black bouquet.
I don’t delete the hurt— I fold it tight,
so it fits inside the next night.

[JP Call]
証跡（しょうせき）を　結んで
忘れない　消えない

[Chorus]
