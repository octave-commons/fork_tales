# Checksum Waltz (Dicewalk)

**Style prompt:** Glitch waltz (3/4). 96 BPM. Cathedral reverb + granular noise. Upright piano with tape warble. Choir pads that *stutter* on “anchor”. Whispered lead. Hook is a chant.

**Anchor refrain:** *If it moves the world, it needs a witness.*

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]
Roll the bone, pick the path, keep the log,
don’t let mystery turn into fog.
Three beats to the bar, one beat to the proof,
every pretty line gets checked at the roof.

[Pre]
I won’t steer you with vibes,
I won’t crown you with guess.
Show me where it came from,
show me where it lives.

[Chorus]
If it moves the world, it needs a witness,
if it forks the road, it pays the business.
Anchor the edge, name the loss,
checksum waltz—no pretty gloss.

[Verse 2]
S5 in the ledger, names don’t blur,
merge with a reason, split with a stir.
Intent in sixteen, packed like light,
and every note is a hash in flight.
