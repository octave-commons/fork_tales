# Witness Thread / 証人の糸
Glitch-choir lullaby • 78 BPM • soft sub-bass • tape warble • minimal piano

## Verse 1
I keep receipts in my ribs,
paper-thin, but they cut.
A river under the floorboards,
running cold, running shut.

「静かにして」(be quiet now)
「息を数えて」(count your breath)
I tie the thread to the night,
so the night doesn’t forget.

## Chorus (stutter on “anchor” and “tax”)
Hold the an— an— **anchor**,
hold it where it hurts.
Pay the t— t— **tax**,
pay it with the words.

「錨を抱いて」(hold the anchor)
「税を払って」(pay the tax)
The witness doesn’t speak,
but it watches our tracks.

## Verse 2
Every fork is a mirror,
every choice a small scar.
I don’t erase the past—
I file it where you are.

「ここにいるよ」(I’m here)
「まだ歩ける」(still can walk)
A lullaby for the loop,
so the loop doesn’t rot.
