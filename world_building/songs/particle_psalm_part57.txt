# Particle Psalm / 粒子の詩

The Mage of Receipts doesn’t cast fire.
They cast *state*.

A hundred little bodies (position, velocity, mass),
each one a syllable you can’t argue with.
They circle the anchor not because they believe,
but because the field makes belief unnecessary.

When the world approaches its limit,
we don’t delete—  
we braid overlays,
and let the originals sleep.

η is the witness you cannot summon.
μ is the footprint you cannot deny.
Between them: a simulation that keeps running,
until the story remembers how to breathe.
