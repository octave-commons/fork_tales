# Fork Tax Epic / 分岐税 叙事詩
Mythic glitch epic • spoken-sung • 84 BPM • cathedral reverb • heartbeat sub

## Section I — The Ledger Gate
In the cathedral of caches,
I kneel to no king—
I kneel to the proof
of the thing I can bring.

η is the gaze you can’t own.
μ is the work you can’t fake.
I pay the fork tax in public
so the private won’t break.

「最後の言葉で答える」(answer on the last word)
