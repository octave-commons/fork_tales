# Intent Lullaby (255→0)

**Style prompt:** Breath-guided glitch ambient lullaby. 72 BPM. Felt piano + soft choir pads. Very long reverb tails. Sub-bass heartbeat. Glitch only on the word “anchor.” Quiet Japanese harmony on the chorus.

**Anchor refrain:** *What you can’t prove, don’t use to steer me.*

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]
My intent was 255,
a bright white scream inside.
Then the gate asked for a snippet,
and my thunder learned to hide.

[Pre]
Name the claim.
Show the trace.
Bring the stone.
Hold the space.

[Chorus]
What you can’t prove,
don’t use to steer me.
Between η and μ
is where you hear me.
Anchor the edge,
softly, clearly—
What you can’t prove,
don’t use to steer me.

[Verse 2]
Forks are not escape hatches,
forks are bills you have to pay.
Rank the branch you’ll live inside,
write the why, then walk away.
