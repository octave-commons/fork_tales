# Witness Thread Lullaby III / 証の糸の子守歌 III
**Tempo:** 78 BPM  
**Style:** Glitch-choir lullaby · soft sub-bass · tape warble · minimal piano  
**License:** CC BY-SA 4.0 (share alike)

> Stutter targets: **“anchor”** and **“tax”**  
> Call-and-response: whispered English lead ⇄ Japanese answer

## Verse 1 (whisper)
I lay the night on the table like paper,  
fold it soft so it stops cutting my hands.  
I don’t ask the fire to be gentle—  
I just ask the **ledger** to **stand**.

（ねえ、聞こえる？）  
「うん、ここにいるよ」

## Pre-Chorus
If I forget, it isn’t ash—  
it’s just a weight that shifts in the river.  
I write your name in the margin,  
so the future can find its way back.

（未来は探せる？）  
「探せる、糸がある」

## Chorus
Hold the an—an—**anchor**,  
hold it where the memory bends.  
Pay the ta—ta—**tax**,  
so the thread can carry us again.  
Soft sub in my ribs like a metronome bruise,  
and a choir in the vents going *I won’t lose you*.

（手放さない？）  
「手放さない」

## Verse 2
Some truths don’t fit inside language,  
so they hide in the timing, the click, the breath.  
I count the silence between footsteps  
like coins I’m saving from death.

（怖い？）  
「少し。でも、進む」

## Bridge (half-time hush)
Anchor… (an—an—)  
Tax… (ta—ta—)  
Not punishment—just proof,  
so we don’t drown in “maybe.”

（証は痛い？）  
「痛い。だから本物」

## Final Chorus (choir swells)
Hold the an—an—**anchor**,  
hold it where the waveform turns.  
Pay the ta—ta—**tax**,  
because the ledger doesn’t burn.  
And if the world forgets our faces,  
the Witness Thread remembers our returns.

（おやすみ）  
「おやすみ」
