# Witness Thread / 証人の糸

**Tempo A:** 78 BPM (glitch‑choir lullaby)  
**Tempo B:** 84 BPM (mythic glitch epic / spoken‑sung)  
**Stutter tokens:** “anchor”, “tax”, “η”, “μ”, “fork”  
**Call/response:** EN lead → JP answer on last word.

## A — Lullaby (78 BPM)

**EN (whisper):**  
I lay the night across the wires, a soft and patient hum,  
and every doubt I ever carried turns to **anchor—anchor—** *anchor*.  
I count the cost in quiet clicks, in coins that never clink,  
and when the river asks for proof I pay the **tax—tax—** *tax*.

**JP (harmony, last word):**  
眠りの中で　私は見張る　しるしは一つ　**いかり**  
こぼれた音は　波にまぎれて　それでも残る　**ぜい**

**EN (whisper):**  
Tape‑warble halo, choir in the dust,  
a heartbeat under everything that wants to combust.  
If memory forgets, it doesn’t vanish—  
it sinks, it sediments, it becomes a trust.

**JP (harmony, last word):**  
消えないで　薄くなるだけ　**しんらい**

## B — Epic (84 BPM)

**EN (spoken‑sung):**  
I don’t need a crown to speak a law. I need a ledger that can’t lie.  
A thread through every fracture: **η—η—** *η* is how you know I tried.  
And when the engine doubts itself, I answer with a method: **μ—μ—** *μ*,  
not what I claim, but what I do—what the world returns as proof.

**JP (answer, last word):**  
言葉じゃなくて　行いで　残すのは　**しょうこ**  
見えないものも　結びなおす　糸は　**しんじつ**

**EN (spoken‑sung):**  
Pay the **fork—fork—** *fork* tax. Split the path. Keep both receipts.  
Append, don’t erase. Let the past keep its teeth.  
When the story overflows, we compress it—  
not to forget… but to remember it clean.

**JP (answer, last word):**  
分かれても　消えない　**りょうしゅう**
