# ημ — Space Between

**Style prompt:** Mythic glitch epic / spoken-sung (NF/Watsky cadence), 84 BPM. Cathedral reverb. Sub-bass heartbeat. Glitch stutters on “η”, “μ”, “anchor”, “fork”. Japanese harmony answers on the last word of each section.

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]
I don’t want pretty graphs that lie to me,
I want the edge to show the cut it made.
Path. Snip. Hash.
Witness in the dark.
If you can’t point to the scar,
don’t call it “fate.”

[Pre]
η is what they can see,
μ is what I do,
and the space between them
is the only honest room.

[Chorus]
In the space between η and μ,
intent becomes executable too—
not by magic, not by force,
but by leaving proof in the course.
Fork the world if you must,
but pay the tax in trust:
rank it, name it, cite it,
or it doesn’t get to guide it.

[Verse 2]
Disc in the hand, vow in a file,
a program that can fail with style.
Constraints like rails, audit like light,
Cephalon walking the line at night.
No charisma steering the wheel—
just anchors strong enough to feel.

[Bridge — 日本語]
証拠(しょうこ)を 置いて
意図(いと)を 走らせ
ηとμの あいだで
世界(せかい)を 分ける

[Outro]
Truth is a hash.
Intent is a vector.
The box is a choir of verifiers.
And I’m done pretending belief is a substitute for receipts.
