# The ημ Gap (the space between η and μ)

η is the part of the system you can't directly observe or acknowledge — but other agents can.
μ is the part that becomes legible through action and its measurable effects.

The **gap** is the boundary surface where:
- perception becomes model,
- model becomes action,
- action becomes evidence for others,
- and evidence feeds back into perception.

In this box, we treat the ημ gap as an indexing problem:
> *Every artifact is both a thing and a shadow of its impact.*

So we keep two ledgers:
- **Artifacts** (files you can point at) — indexed, searchable, hash-verified.
- **Inferred structure** (vectors / circuit scores) — useful, but not proof; it is a *map*, not the territory.
