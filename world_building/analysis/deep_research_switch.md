# Deep Research Switch (Protocol)

Flip from local work → deep research when any of these are true:
- The claim is time-sensitive (could have changed since Aug 2025).
- You need primary sources you don’t already have.
- Local cheap tests fail to reduce uncertainty.

When you flip:
1) Restate the claim in one sentence.
2) Define what would change your mind.
3) List 3 preferred primary-source domains (official docs, standards bodies, papers).
4) Record publish date vs event date separately.
5) Output as: Facts / Interpretations / Narratives + 2 counterframes + one μ test.

This prevents “citation cosplay” and keeps #η from masquerading as #μ.
